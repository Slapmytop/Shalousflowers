I am a qualified freelance florist and floral designer based in cleethorpes, ne lincs. I am passionate about flowers and good design. I only use the freshest materials of the highest quality to ensure that the flowers you receive will give you many days of enjoyment well beyond your special day.

I offer a truly exceptional service tailor-made to suit your budget and your individual needs. As part of this service I offer a 'free, no obligation' Wedding Consultation. With my passion and enthusiasm I will always strive to create the most exquisite, elegant and bespoke floral designs with a 'wow' factor that is totally fitting to your personalities.

I work with all kinds of budgets, whether your celebration is small and intimate, or large and extravagant your day will be perfect in every way.

Whatever the occasion I strive to create stunning, bespoke floral designs using a variety of materials and accessories that are totally fitting to your requirements and your day.

I also provide corporate work, sympathy designs, bouquets, gifts or other special occasion arrangements.
